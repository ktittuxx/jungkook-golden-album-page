JUNGKOOK of BTS

Jungkook’s album GOLDEN signifies his "golden moments" as a solo artist and references his nickname "Golden Maknae" (golden youngest) given by BTS members, highlighting his all-rounder talent in singing, dancing, and producing.

About Jungkook

Jungkook is a South Korean singer and member of BTS. He is known for his powerful vocals, dancing skills, and versatility as a performer.

Song Meanings

3D

A song about wanting to see a partner in real life. The singer expresses affection and desire but respects the other person’s consent and feelings.

Closer To You

The singer promises loyalty and support in a relationship. Even when difficulties appear, he believes those challenges will only bring them closer together.

Standing Next To You

A strong declaration of love and commitment. The singer promises to stay with his partner forever, describing a deep love that cannot be broken.

Yes Or No

The singer finally starts a relationship but feels unsure about the other person’s feelings. He asks for a clear answer about whether they should continue together.

Please Don't Change

The singer fears losing the relationship and asks his partner to stay the same. He also promises that his own feelings will remain sincere and unchanged.

Hate You

A painful breakup song. The singer prefers to hate his ex-partner rather than face the deep sadness caused by losing the relationship.

Somebody

After the breakup, the singer expresses resentment. He hopes his former lover finds someone else, making it clear their relationship is truly over.

Too Sad To Dance

The singer feels lonely and heartbroken after the breakup. He struggles to enjoy life until advice from his father encourages him to move forward.

Shot Glass of Tears

The singer reflects on a painful love and tries to escape his sadness through drinking, showing the emptiness left by the breakup.

Seven

Placed at the end of the album, the song suggests that after heartbreak people eventually try to love again, showing the repeating cycle of relationships.
